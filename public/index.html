OUR STORY

Rooted in Nature, Refined by Tradition

Nestled in the sun-drenched valleys of Valandovo, Makedon Sun was born from a deep respect for nature, tradition, and the unique power of the persimmon fruit. What began as a family passion grew into a winery and distillery unlike any other — blending time-honored techniques with modern standards to craft wines, rakija, mastika, and vinegar from the rare Diospyros kaki (Japanese persimmon). Each bottle we produce carries the story of our land: the rich soil, the golden sun, and generations of knowledge passed down with care. From the vineyard to your glass, Makedon Sun is a symbol of authentic Macedonian spirit, quality, and innovation.

Persimmon - Nature's Golden Fruit

Persimmon (Diospyros kaki), often called the Japanese apple, is not only delicious but also packed with valuable nutrients. Rich in vitamins A, C, and E, as well as antioxidants, potassium, and dietary fiber, this fruit supports the immune system, promotes healthy digestion, and helps protect the body from oxidative stress.

Its high content of beta-carotene contributes to healthy vision and radiant skin, while the natural antioxidants support heart health and overall vitality. Low in fat and cholesterol-free, persimmon is considered a true superfruit – a perfect balance of taste and wellness in every bite.
