ABOUT US

We are a relatively new, family-owned winery and distillery from Valandovo, the warmest region in Macedonia. Our name – MAKEDON SUN – was inspired by the strong and radiant Macedonian sun, a true symbol of our land and heritage. Guided by tradition, passion, and innovation, we craft authentic wines, spirits, and unique products made from Japanese apples, grown in one of the most fertile and sun-kissed areas of the country. Every drop reflects the power of nature and the spirit of Macedonia.

Makedon Sun - Winery & Distillery

Makedon Sun is a premium brand under the company Andi Kom from North Macedonia, specializing in the production of wine, rakija, mastika, and vinegar made from persimmon (Japanese apple). Our winery and distillery combine tradition with modern technology to craft natural alcoholic beverages with a unique taste.

We are the only producer in our region – and in fact in all of Europe – that creates vinegar and alcoholic drinks from persimmon. This extraordinary fruit, known for its health benefits and rich flavor, is at the heart of our story.

Our product portfolio includes:

  • Premium wines with a modern and elegant design
  • Rakija with authentic Macedonian character
  • Mastika as a symbol of tradition, crafted in a contemporary way
  • Persimmon vinegar – the only one of its kind in Europe

Our mission is to bring the power of nature in every drop, offering consumers natural, high-quality, and authentic beverages that tell the story of Macedonia.

Makedon Sun is the choice for lovers of premium spirits, fine wines, and natural beverages who are looking for more than just a drink – they are looking for an experience.
